# Nexari OmniHub — Pricing

**Last updated: June 2, 2026**

All prices are in Canadian Dollars (CAD) unless otherwise noted. Prices exclude applicable taxes.
Annual billing available on all plans: **2 months free (~16% savings)**.
All plans include a **60-day free trial — no credit card required (3 screens)**.

---

## Billing System Architecture

### Trial Policy
- **Default**: 60 days, 3 screens, no credit card required
- Superadmin can extend trial duration and screen limit for any org
- Management companies (SIs) can extend trials for their own client orgs

### Screen Metering
- Real-time metered billing — you are charged for the screens you have active each billing cycle
- Screen count changes take effect immediately

### Currencies Supported
- CAD (primary), USD, GBP, EUR, AUD
- Prices shown in CAD unless your account is set to a different currency

### Billing Models for Resellers / SIs
Nexari offers two models — management companies can choose based on their preference:

| Model | How it works |
|---|---|
| **Reseller** | Nexari invoices the SI at a wholesale rate; SI bills their clients independently |
| **Direct** | Nexari bills the client directly via Stripe; SI earns a commission paid by Nexari |
| **Flexible** | SI chooses a model per client (default for new management company accounts) |

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I mix Basic and Pro screens in the same organization?**
No. The signage plan applies at the organization level. Upgrade to Pro to access SyncPlay and video wall features across all screens in that organization.

**Does the Menu Board plan include the signage dashboard?**
The Menu Board plan provides device management, monitoring, and scheduling for menu board screens. It does not include SyncPlay or video wall features. Add a signage bundle to get those.

**Can I upgrade from Menu Board to a bundle mid-billing cycle?**
Yes. Upgrades are prorated and take effect immediately.

**Is there a setup fee?**
No setup fees on any self-serve plan. Assisted onboarding is available for enterprise and reseller accounts.

**What POS systems are supported?**
Square, Toast, Lightspeed, and Clover at launch. Additional connectors on the roadmap. Contact support for integration status.

**Are prices in USD or CAD?**
All listed prices are in Canadian Dollars (CAD). USD and other currency billing available — contact sales.
