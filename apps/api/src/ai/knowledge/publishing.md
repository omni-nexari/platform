# Publishing

Publishing tells devices what to play. A device can have one primary published target at a time: content, playlist, schedule, or sync group.

## Publish one content item
1. Go to **Content**.
2. Select one content item.
3. Click **Publish**.
4. Choose the mode:
   - **Single** for normal full-screen playback on selected devices or groups.
   - **Videowall** for cropped playback across a video wall group.
5. Select devices or groups.
6. Confirm Publish.

## Publish to all signage screens
For many screens, create a reusable device group:
1. Go to **Device Groups**.
2. Create a group named "All Screens" or similar.
3. Add every target signage device in the workspace.
4. Publish content, a playlist, or a schedule to that group.

For one-off content publishing, the Content publish wizard can select multiple individual devices. For recurring operations, a device group is easier.

## Publish playlists and schedules
- Publish a **playlist** when the screens should play a loop immediately.
- Publish a **schedule** when time rules should decide what plays.
- Publish to individual devices from the Devices area, or publish to a Device Group for bulk changes.

## Publish synchronized playback
- Use a **Sync Playlist** when multiple screens should play the same content in lockstep.
- Publish the Sync Playlist to selected screens. Nexari creates or reuses a sync group and points devices at it.

## Publish video walls
- Use **Videowall** mode from Content when one full-wall asset should be cropped across panels.
- Use a **Video Wall playlist** when each panel should receive its own content.
- A video wall group needs a saved grid layout before publishing.

## Unpublish
Unpublish clears the device-level published content, playlist, schedule, or sync group. The device returns to normal workspace schedule and fallback behavior.

## Good answer
"To publish to all screens, create a Device Group that contains all target signage devices, then publish content, a playlist, or a schedule to that group. For one-off content, you can also open Content, click Publish, choose Single mode, and select multiple devices directly."
